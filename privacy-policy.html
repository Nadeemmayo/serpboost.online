Privacy Policy

SERPBoost respects your privacy and protects the information you share with us for SEO, guest posting, link building and digital marketing services.

Last Updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SERPBoost collects, uses, stores and protects information provided by website visitors, clients, prospects and business partners.

Information We Collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide when contacting us, submitting a website form, requesting a quotation or ordering a service.

  • Your name, business name and contact information.
  • Your email address, telephone number or messaging account.
  • Your website URL, business niche and target market.
  • Your target keywords, landing pages and project requirements.
  • Information contained in messages, forms or project documents.
  • Billing and payment-related information required to process an approved order.

We may also collect limited technical information automatically, including browser type, device type, approximate location, referral source, pages visited and website interaction data.

How We Use Your Information

Information is used only for legitimate business, communication, service-delivery and website-improvement purposes.

  • To respond to inquiries and consultation requests.
  • To understand your SEO goals, market and website requirements.
  • To prepare guest posting, backlink, niche-edit or SEO recommendations.
  • To process orders and provide approved services.
  • To communicate project updates, reports and placement information.
  • To provide customer service and technical support.
  • To prevent spam, abuse, fraud and unauthorized activity.
  • To improve website usability, content and service quality.

Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable, we process information because it is necessary to respond to your request, provide a contracted service, comply with legal obligations or support our legitimate business interests.

Where consent is required, you may withdraw that consent by contacting us. Withdrawing consent does not affect processing that occurred before the withdrawal.

Data Protection

SERPBoost takes reasonable administrative, organizational and technical measures to protect personal and project information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration or disclosure.

Although we apply reasonable safeguards, no online transmission or electronic storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Client Confidentiality

Website URLs, keyword plans, backlink strategies, target pages, reports, business information and campaign details shared with SERPBoost are treated as confidential project information.

We do not publicly disclose client information or confidential campaign details without permission, except where disclosure is required by law.

Third-Party Service Providers

We may use trusted third-party platforms to operate our website, process forms, communicate with clients, manage projects, analyze website performance or process payments.

These providers may receive only the information reasonably required to perform their services. Their handling of information is governed by their own privacy policies and legal obligations.

Publisher and Placement Information

When a client approves guest posting, niche edits, digital PR or another placement service, limited project information may be shared with relevant publishers or service partners.

This may include article content, website URL, target URL, suggested anchor text, brand information or other details needed to complete the approved placement.

Payments

Payments may be processed through third-party payment providers such as PayPal, Payoneer, card-processing platforms or bank-transfer services.

SERPBoost does not normally store complete payment-card details. Payment information is handled by the selected payment provider under its own security and privacy practices.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies, analytics tools or similar technologies to remember preferences, understand website performance and improve the visitor experience.

You may block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain website functions.

External Website Links

SERPBoost may link to third-party websites, publishers, social platforms, payment services or other external resources.

We do not control and are not responsible for the content, security or privacy practices of external websites. Visitors should review the privacy policy of any third-party website they use.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to inquiries, deliver services, maintain business records, resolve disputes and meet legal or accounting obligations.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information, the service provided and applicable legal requirements.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights concerning your personal information.

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of information where legally permitted.
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Request a portable copy of eligible information.

To submit a privacy request, contact us using the details provided below. We may need to verify your identity before completing a request.

Children's Privacy

SERPBoost services are intended for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the applicable legal age.

Contact us if you believe a child has submitted personal information so that we can review and remove it where appropriate.

International Services

SERPBoost serves clients worldwide. Information may be processed or stored in countries different from your own, including through international service providers.

We take reasonable steps to work with reputable providers and protect information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Policy Updates

SERPBoost may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our website, services, business practices or legal requirements.

Updated versions will be published on this page. The latest revision date will appear near the top of the policy.
