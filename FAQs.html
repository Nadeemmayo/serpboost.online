General SEO Questions

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of improving your website's visibility in search engines like Google through technical optimization, quality content, keyword targeting and authority building.
SEO helps increase organic traffic, improve online visibility, generate qualified leads and build long-term business growth without relying only on paid advertising.
On-Page SEO focuses on website content, titles, meta tags, speed and internal optimization, while Off-Page SEO focuses on backlinks, guest posting, digital PR and authority building.
SEO is a long-term strategy. Most websites begin seeing noticeable improvements within 3 to 6 months depending on competition, website history and optimization quality.
